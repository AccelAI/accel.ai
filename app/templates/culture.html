Blue Word AI

Blue Word AI focuses on understanding intentions within human language by analyzing words in their context through people's digital content such as emails, social accounts and online searches. The algorithms are based on constructing vector representations of words and creating semantic groups. BlueWordAi technology aims to teach the machine to recognize not only keywords but the intentions within the human language. Real life applications are chatbots that will improve communication skills of the users and enable Ai-backed customer service and sales centers.
